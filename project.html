VISIT SITE

The Age of Aerospace

Discovery Channel / Boeing

Role
Lead UX/Visual Designer

The Age of Aerospace explores the last 100 years of aviation history in unprecedented detail. Working alongside Boeing and "The Documentary Group", I was tasked with the architecture and visual design of a robust multi-screen global digital experience. The website, which has been fully translated into 17 languages, is made up of interactive episodes and over 300 pieces of interactive content. The project has garnered the recognition of prestigious awards such as The Webby Awards, the iab mixx awards and the Digiday Awards.
